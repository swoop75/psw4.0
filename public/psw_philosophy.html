My Investment Philosophy

A Disciplined Approach to Dividend Investing

Welcome to my dividend investing journey—this is PSW: Pengamaskinen Sverige + Worldwide.

At the heart of my strategy is a simple goal: building consistent income through dividends. My portfolio includes more than 500 companies, all selected through a highly disciplined, data-driven approach.

I don't chase market predictions or get caught up in short-term noise. Emotions and gut feelings take a backseat. Instead, I've designed a structured system that ranks and scores companies, guiding every buy (and the occasional sell) based on clear, predefined rules. I only trade on weekends to stay focused and keep daily market swings from clouding my judgment.

Rather than trimming positions, I prefer to channel new cash and reinvested dividends into smaller holdings. It's all about steady, reliable growth—and for me, dividends are what it's all about.

Highlights of the PSW Approach

  • Focus on Dividends & Income: My core goal is to build a steady stream of income.
  • Data-Driven System: Investment decisions are based on objective rules and scoring, not predictions.
  • Disciplined & Emotion-Free: Orders are placed only on weekends to avoid market distractions and emotional trading.
  • Long-Term Growth: I rarely sell and reinvest all new cash and dividends into smaller positions to maximize portfolio growth.
  • Global Reach: Investments span across the globe, not limited to any single market.
  • Over 500 Companies: A highly diversified portfolio designed for resilience.
Freedom level: > 100% FI(RE). Committed to achieving complete financial independence through dividends.
