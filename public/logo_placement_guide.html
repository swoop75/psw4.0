    Where to Add Your Logo

    To implement your custom logo, simply replace the <i class="fas fa-chart-line"></i> icons with:


    For image logos: <img src="your-logo.png" alt="PSW Logo">

    For SVG logos: Paste your SVG code directly

    For icon fonts: Use your preferred icon library classes


    The circular gradient background will automatically frame your logo beautifully!
